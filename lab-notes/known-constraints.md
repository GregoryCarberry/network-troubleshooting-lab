# Known Constraints

This lab balances experimentation with day-to-day home usability.

## Current Constraints

- VLAN 99 currently reachable from VLAN 10 for practical admin access.
- Powerline adapters do not reliably preserve tagged VLAN traffic.
- Wireless roaming tuning not yet optimised for all AP combinations.

## Intent

These are documented states, not oversights.

They represent either:
- Tradeoffs for home stability
- Hardware limitations
- Planned future hardening steps
