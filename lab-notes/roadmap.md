# Lab Roadmap

This lab is designed to evolve.

## Short Term

- Tighten VLAN 99 access to host-based restrictions.
- Improve documentation of trunk validation workflows.
- Add monitoring and logging visibility.

## Medium Term

- Introduce least-privilege management access model.
- Expand wireless optimisation testing.
- Compare routing centralisation vs distributed L3 switching.

## Long Term

- Introduce automation where appropriate.
- Document hardware lifecycle decisions.
- Continue iterative refinement through controlled failure scenarios.
